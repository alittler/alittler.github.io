Hi, I'm Andrew

After surviving an accident with the business-end of a sedan, I have spent the past decade or two working on my recovery through writing. I have been writing genre fiction for years, and have been reading genre fictions for years more.

When I am not working on one book, I am brainstorming another over my regular drink at my local Starbucks and researching the shape of magic. When I am not studying the speed of historic forms of transportation, I am theorizing about interdimentional deities. When I am not analyzing the design of libraries in Rio de Janeiro, I am looking up the recipes of pioneer settlements in Canada.

From a novel about talking animals and deistic trees, to one about a barren rabbit running away from her warren.
